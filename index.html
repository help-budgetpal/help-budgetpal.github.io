How it works

Expense tracking in 4 simple steps

01

Select your spreadsheet

Using the Google File Picker, select your monthly expense tracking spreadsheet.

02

Browse for your bank CSV files

Select the month, bank name and CSV file that you want to upload. You can select up to 10 files at the same time.

03

Customize your categories

Take advantage of BudgetPal's auto-categorize feature by defining a keyword and its category. You will only have to do this once, they will be remembered for future months!

04

Review and Upload

Look over all the transactions that have been processed - optionally, you can choose to deselect a transaction or edit its description. When everything looks good, press upload to see your transactions appear in your monthly budgeting spreadsheet.
